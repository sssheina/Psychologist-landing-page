01 ABOUT ME

Let's get acquainted

I'm Rina and I recently moved to Israel with my dog Toby. Moving to

a new country, I had to start over again from scratch. It's not easy and I'm still adjusting.

I've been through a lot: losing my parents early on, dealing with bullying at school, struggling with bulimia, struggling to get out of an abusive relationship, being treated in a psychiatric hospital with severe depression...

When I was undergoing treatment, I felt completely hopeless - nothing was helping anymore. At that point, I started reading books about cognitive behavioural therapy and began applying it — and it literally saved my life.

I then decided to retrain from being
a Japanese language teacher to become
a psychologist at the National Research Institute for Further Education and Professional Training.

After graduating, I studied CBT and other psychological approaches in more detail at the CBT Association, the Metaphora Centre for Further Education in Psychology and also attended an Oxford course.

* CBT (Cognitive Behavioural Therapy) is a psychological approach based on the analysis and adjustment of one's own beliefs and thoughts.

10 FAQ

FAQ

Is it possible to pay with a Russian card?

Yes, you can pay with a Russian card in Russian roubles. The 50€ amount should be converted at the current exchange rate.

What do I do if I can't articulate my request, if
I just I don't know what's going on?

If you find it difficult to formulate your enquiry, that is normal. Come to a counselling session and we'll formulate your needs and goals together. That's part of my job. Don't be afraid to come in, I'm here to help you.

What do I do if I don't know how to start

a conversation in counselling?

At the orientation session, we'll get to know each other. I'm going to ask you questions. It's a live process, but you can lean on me, I'll help and guide you.

How often should I attend counselling sessions?

I recommend attending counselling sessions at least once every fortnight. I believe that this is the minimum frequency that will give you the benefits of my counselling sessions.

How much counselling will I need?

The number of counselling sessions is individual, depending on many factors.

How can I cancel or reschedule my consultation if something changes in
my schedule?

You can reschedule or cancel a consultation up to 24 hours in advance without charge. Otherwise, you will be required to pay for the session. Please note that if you are late, the consultation time will not be extended.

What if I can't continue to attend counselling?

Don't worry, even if at some point you're unable to attend counselling, you'll always have a set of effective tools for self-help that you will receive in our counselling sessions.
